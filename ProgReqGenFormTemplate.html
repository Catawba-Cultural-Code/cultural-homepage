Program Agreements

Assurances

Catawba Nation’s Cultural Division (“Cultural Center”) agrees to provide an educational program (“Program”) for {{groupName}} (“Partner Org.”) conforming to the details listed in the Program Details section. Prior to signing this agreement, both parties shall have confirmed the information listed in the Program Details.

The Partner Org. agrees to pay the Cultural Center in accordance with the included Invoice. The Cultural Center retains the right to charge the Partner Org. for damages to facilities or collections and other costs incurred in the course of their program.

The Partner Org is responsible for any and all transportation to and from the Cultural Center or other Program site.

The Partner Org. is responsible for all translation accommodations. Cultural Center staff will be available for interpreters to explain context-specific language prior to the Program.

The Partner Org. agrees to all of the following behavioral expectations. If a group includes people under the age of 18, the Partner Org. is required to secure parental/guardian consent and provide adequate chaperones to enforce these expectations.


Behavioral Expectations

  • Respect People: All visitors to the Cultural Center are expected to treat staff, community members, and other visitors with respect. This includes listening respectfully to elders and other presenters. Anyone threatening another person, including using harassing language, will be asked to leave followed by appropriate action as determined by Catawba Nation.
  • Respect Place: The Cultural Center and the objects protected inside represent an important history for Catawba Nation. We ask that visitors respect this by not touching exhibits unless directed to do so, staying within public areas, and treating our facilities with care.
  • Respect Land: Please refrain from littering at the Cultural Center or on surrounding lands. Please do not harvest, destroy, remove, or otherwise move any plant, animal, mineral, or other item without explicit permission from the Cultural Center. We invite every visitor to help us respect the land by removing litter where safe and possible.

Cancellation Policy

The Cultural Center requests that cancellation or rescheduling notice be given at least 48 hours before the scheduled program. Without adequate notice, groups may still be liable for a full or partial payment of program fees.
